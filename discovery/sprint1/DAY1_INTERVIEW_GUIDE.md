# Day 1 Interview Guide (Sprint 1)

Owner: Phil  
Mission: Generate decision-grade discovery evidence for pilot scope and GTM positioning.

## Interview Design Rules

- Duration: 35-45 minutes
- Recording: only with consent
- Evidence quality: capture direct quotes and concrete examples
- Avoid solutioning in-session; probe workflow reality first
- Keep in scope: compliance-analysis workflow only
- Explicitly tag each finding as pilot-critical or nice-to-have

## Core Questions (All Personas)

1. Walk me through your import compliance workflow from shipment creation to broker handoff.
2. Which tools or spreadsheets are used at each step?
3. Where do delays or errors happen most often?
4. What are the top three failure modes you worry about?
5. What output must be present before you trust a system recommendation?
6. What would make you reject a new platform, even if features look useful?
7. How do you evaluate whether a workflow improved your result?
8. If this worked well, what KPI would change first?

## Persona-Specific Probes

### A) Import Compliance Leaders (Primary)

- How do you validate eligibility/refusal decisions today?
- What creates audit risk in your current review process?
- What review output format is mandatory for leadership or legal sign-off?
- What is your threshold for relying on a system-generated analysis?
- What implementation friction would block adoption this quarter?

### B) Customs Brokers (Secondary)

- What do you need from importers to begin broker-prep reliably?
- Which upstream data quality issues cause rework?
- What export format/content reduces broker turnaround time?
- Where do importer handoffs fail most frequently?
- Which blocker types should prevent export automatically?

### C) SMB Import Owners (Tertiary)

- What part of compliance-analysis is most confusing or time-consuming?
- What result do you need to feel "safe enough to proceed"?
- What support or explanation is required to trust a recommendation?
- What budget/time constraints limit adoption?
- What is your minimum acceptable setup burden?
