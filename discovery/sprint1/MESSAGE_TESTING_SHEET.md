## Testing Method

1. Use one baseline and one alternative message per interview.
2. Ask participant to restate value proposition in their own words.
3. Capture confusion points, credibility gaps, and objection triggers.
4. Tag each response by persona and confidence level.
5. Promote language only when supported by repeated quotes.

## Promotion Criteria

- Message variant accepted by at least two interviews in target segment
- Objection rate lower than baseline framing
- Linkable to pilot KPI outcomes (conversion confidence or completion confidence)
