# Top 5 Pilot Risks From Discovery (Draft v0)

Owner: Phil  
Due: End of Sprint 1 Week  
Status: Draft to be updated with interview evidence

Note: This memo is a Day 1 draft hypothesis list. Convert each item to evidence-backed form before final submission.

## Risk 1: Trust Threshold for Analysis Output Is Higher Than Current Output Design

- Why this matters: If users cannot verify reasoning quickly, they may not rely on NECO outputs.
- Potential KPI impact:
  - Pilot path completion rate down
  - Export success rate down
- Evidence needed this week:
  - Repeated statements on required output components
  - Required fields for reviewer confidence

## Risk 2: Upstream Data Quality Gaps Break Broker-Prep Handoff

- Why this matters: Missing or inconsistent data can block the downstream broker workflow.
- Potential KPI impact:
  - Failed analyses up
  - Broker-prep export success rate down
- Evidence needed this week:
  - Broker examples of rework triggers
  - Importer-side data collection failure points

## Risk 3: Segment Misalignment on "Must-Have" Outcomes

- Why this matters: Importers, brokers, and SMBs may prioritize different outputs, causing roadmap conflict.
- Potential KPI impact:
  - Lower conversion in one or more segments
  - Slower completion rate due to workflow mismatch
- Evidence needed this week:
  - Side-by-side must-have matrix across segments
  - Contradictions with attributable quotes

## Risk 4: Adoption Blockers Are Operational, Not Functional

- Why this matters: Even correct functionality may fail if onboarding burden is too high.
- Potential KPI impact:
  - Interviews convert to pilots at low rates
  - Time-to-first-analysis worsens
- Evidence needed this week:
  - Implementation objection frequency
  - Minimum acceptable setup expectations

## Risk 5: Evidence Quality Drift Causes Weak Prioritization Decisions

- Why this matters: Recommendations without traceable evidence can create silent scope drift.
- Potential KPI impact:
  - Sprint throughput volatility
  - Scope changes without KPI benefit
- Evidence needed this week:
  - Completeness checks for interview schema
  - Evidence-to-decision traceability coverage

## End-of-Week Submission Rules

- No recommendation without:
  - Attributable evidence
  - KPI impact statement
  - Pilot-critical or nice-to-have label
- Explicitly flag any signal that implies:
  - scope change,
  - launch assumption change, or
  - ICP focus change.
