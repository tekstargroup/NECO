# Sprint 1 Interview Pipeline (Target: 5-8)

Owner: Phil  
Window: Sprint 1

## Segment Mix Target

- 3-4 Import Compliance leaders (primary)
- 1-2 Customs brokers (secondary)
- 1-2 SMB import owners (tertiary)

## Recruiting and Scheduling Workflow

1. Build target list by segment with company type and import context notes.
2. Send outreach with explicit purpose: improve compliance-analysis pilot path.
3. Offer two scheduling windows and a 35-45 minute session.
4. Confirm recording consent and confidentiality boundaries.
5. Pre-tag each interview by segment priority and evidence gap it should close.
6. Publish confirmed schedule to weekly discovery review notes.

## Daily Operating Cadence

- Daily async status update:
  - Outreach sent
  - Interviews confirmed
  - Interviews completed
  - New blockers or contradictions
- Midweek risk review:
  - Segment coverage gap check
  - Evidence quality check
  - Scope or ICP risk escalation
- End-week progress review:
  - Compare completion vs scorecard Section F
  - Confirm readiness of weekly evidence artifacts
