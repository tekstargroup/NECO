# Interview Guide Final (Sprint 1)

Owner: Phil  
Date: February 19, 2026  
Applies to: Import Compliance Leaders, Customs Brokers, SMB Import Owners

## Objective

Generate decision-grade evidence to sharpen pilot scope and GTM positioning for the compliance-analysis pilot path.

## Guardrails

- In scope: shipment creation, document upload, analysis gating, review output, broker-prep export.
- Out of scope: PSC filing execution, entry/import filing execution.
- No feature recommendation without attributable evidence and explicit KPI impact.

## Session Format

- Duration: 35-45 minutes
- Format: 5 minutes context, 25-30 minutes workflow and pain discovery, 10 minutes trust and adoption blockers
- Evidence requirement: minimum three attributable quotes per interview
- Record only with participant consent

## Core Questions (All Personas)

1. Walk me through your process from shipment setup to broker handoff.
2. Which systems and documents do you rely on at each step?
3. What are the top three points where errors or delays occur?
4. What creates the most compliance risk in your current process?
5. What output must exist before you trust a recommendation?
6. What would block your team from adopting a new platform?
7. How do you judge whether a workflow change is successful?
8. Which KPI would improve first if this workflow worked reliably?

## Persona Modules

### Import Compliance Leaders (Primary)

- How are eligibility/refusal decisions validated today?
- What is required for internal auditability and sign-off?
- Which review outputs are mandatory for leadership confidence?
- What implementation constraints would stop this pilot?

### Customs Brokers (Secondary)

- What importer inputs are required to avoid rework?
- Which missing fields most often break handoff quality?
- What broker-prep export format/content is required?
- Which blocker types should force export refusal?

### SMB Import Owners (Tertiary)

- Which step is hardest to understand or execute?
- What explanation detail is required for trust?
- What onboarding effort is acceptable in week one?
- Which cost/time concerns block purchase intent?
