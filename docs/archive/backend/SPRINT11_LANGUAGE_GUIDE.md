# Sprint 11 - Language Guide (Compliance-Grade)

## Core Principle

Language must sound like:
- CBP
- Accounting
- Compliance
- Regulatory filings

**NOT:**
- Tech
- Marketing
- Startup
- AI/ML

---

## Prohibited Terms

### Never Use These Words:

1. **"AI" / "Artificial Intelligence"**
   - Replace with: "System", "Analysis", "Computation"

2. **"Confidence Score" / "Confidence"**
   - Replace with: "Extraction quality", "Certainty level", "Match strength"

3. **"Recommended" / "Recommendation"**
   - Replace with: "Identified", "Detected", "Observed", "Alternative found"

4. **"Optimized" / "Optimization"**
   - Replace with: "Analyzed", "Computed", "Determined"

5. **"Smart" / "Intelligent"**
   - Replace with: "Deterministic", "Rule-based", "Systematic"

6. **"Machine Learning" / "ML"**
   - Replace with: "Pattern matching", "Rule-based analysis"

7. **"Automated" / "Automatic"**
   - Replace with: "System-generated", "Computed", "Determined"

8. **"Suggest" / "Suggestion"**
   - Replace with: "Identify", "Detect", "Observe"

9. **"Best Match" / "Top Result"**
   - Replace with: "Primary match", "First alternative", "Highest similarity"

10. **"Confident" / "Certain"**
    - Replace with: "High extraction quality", "Unambiguous", "Clear"

## Approved Language Patterns

### Detection & Observation

**Use:**
- "Detected"
- "Observed"
- "Identified"
- "Found"
- "Noted"

**Example:**
- ✅ "Alternative classification identified"
- ✅ "Possible overpayment detected"
- ✅ "PSC risk observed"
- ❌ "AI detected an alternative"
- ❌ "Smart system found a better match"

### Review & Action

**Use:**
- "Requires review"
- "Needs verification"
- "Must be confirmed"
- "Subject to review"

**Example:**
- ✅ "Classification requires review"
- ✅ "Missing data must be confirmed"
- ❌ "We recommend reviewing this"
- ❌ "Smart suggestion: review this"

### Risk & Flags

**Use:**
- "Risk detected"
- "Flag raised"
- "Issue identified"
- "Concern noted"

**Example:**
- ✅ "PSC risk detected"
- ✅ "Missing duty flag raised"
- ❌ "AI flagged a risk"
- ❌ "Smart system detected an issue"

### Alternatives & Options

**Use:**
- "Alternative identified"
- "Other option found"
- "Different classification observed"
- "Additional possibility detected"

**Example:**
- ✅ "Alternative HTS code identified: 6112.20.10.10"
- ✅ "Different classification observed in historical entries"
- ❌ "AI recommends alternative code"
- ❌ "Smart system suggests different classification"

### Money & Savings

**Use:**
- "Potential savings identified"
- "Duty difference detected"
- "Possible overpayment observed"
- "Delta calculated"

**Example:**
- ✅ "Potential duty savings identified: $90.00"
- ✅ "Possible overpayment detected: 1.8%"
- ❌ "AI optimized your duty rate"
- ❌ "Smart system found savings"

### Evidence & Sources

**Use:**
- "Evidence extracted"
- "Source document"
- "Based on"
- "Derived from"

**Example:**
- ✅ "Evidence extracted from Commercial Invoice, page 1"
- ✅ "Based on document analysis"
- ❌ "AI analyzed your documents"
- ❌ "Smart extraction from documents"

## Screen-Specific Language

### Shipment Analysis View

**Section Headers:**
- ✅ "Outcome Summary"
- ✅ "Money Impact"
- ✅ "Risk Summary"
- ✅ "Structural Analysis"
- ✅ "Possible Overpayment Detected"
- ✅ "Document Evidence"
- ✅ "Review Status"
- ✅ "Audit Trail"

**Status Labels:**
- ✅ "REVIEW_REQUIRED"
- ✅ "REVIEWED_ACCEPTED"
- ✅ "REVIEWED_REJECTED"
- ✅ "DRAFT"

**Action Buttons:**
- ✅ "Submit for Review"
- ✅ "Override Classification"
- ✅ "Export Audit Pack"
- ✅ "Export for Broker"

### Broker Handoff

**Headers:**
- ✅ "NECO FILING PREP SUMMARY"
- ✅ "CLASSIFICATION & DUTIES"
- ✅ "QUANTITY & VALUE"
- ✅ "REVIEW STATUS"
- ✅ "DISCLAIMERS"

**Disclaimers:**
- ✅ "This is not a filing. Broker review required before submission."
- ✅ "NECO does not provide legal advice or filing recommendations."
- ✅ "Broker assumes full responsibility for final classification and filing decisions."

### Error Messages

**Use:**
- ✅ "Export blocked: classification not reviewed"
- ✅ "Missing required field: quantity"
- ✅ "Conflict detected: multiple values found"

**Avoid:**
- ❌ "Oops! Something went wrong"
- ❌ "We couldn't process this"
- ❌ "Smart system detected an error"

## Tone Checklist

Before publishing any text, verify:

- [ ] No AI/ML terminology
- [ ] No confidence scores
- [ ] No recommendations
- [ ] No optimization claims
- [ ] No marketing language
- [ ] No startup vibes
- [ ] Sounds like CBP/compliance/accounting
- [ ] Conservative and serious
- [ ] Explicit, not implicit
- [ ] Factual, not interpretive

## Examples: Before & After

### Example 1: Money Impact

**BEFORE:**
> "Our AI has optimized your duty rate! We recommend switching to HTS 6112.20.10.10 for maximum savings. Confidence: 95%"

**AFTER:**
> "Potential duty savings identified. Alternative HTS code 6112.20.10.10 shows a duty rate difference of 1.8% ($90.00). No filing recommendation is made."

### Example 2: Risk Detection

**BEFORE:**
> "Smart system detected a risk! We suggest reviewing this classification."

**AFTER:**
> "PSC risk detected. Historical entries show different chapter usage. Classification requires review."

### Example 3: Evidence Extraction

**BEFORE:**
> "AI extracted the following information from your documents with high confidence."

**AFTER:**
> "Evidence extracted from Commercial Invoice, page 1. Extraction quality: High. Source: Line 12, 'Quantity: 100 PCS'."

### Example 4: Review Status

**BEFORE:**
> "This classification looks good! Our AI is confident it's correct."

**AFTER:**
> "Review Status: REVIEWED_ACCEPTED. Reviewed by: reviewer_1. Date: 2024-01-31. Notes: Classification verified against HTSUS."

---

## Final Rule

**If it feels clever, it's wrong.**

**If it sounds like tech, it's wrong.**

**If it could be marketing copy, it's wrong.**

**If a compliance director wouldn't say it, it's wrong.**
